About

Trim Media works with non-profit organizations and small businesses on the design, development, and production of their identity, websites, and digital media.

Identity Design

Identity encompasses the details of any brand. From logos and colors to comprehensive style guides, we bring your brand to life. We love the opporunity to shape the face and personality of growing brands. We get to know our clients and explore their mission, establishing a foundation for their identity to be built on.

Modern Websites

Having an online presence is the single most important aspect of any brand strategy. We work with our clients to understand their goals online. By understanding these goals, we can translate them into actionable metrics, directly influencing the design process for each and every pixel in a website. Our approach means success that can be seen, felt, and celebrated. We love celebrating!

Digital Media

It can be difficult to create a website or social media campaign without photo and video. We have experience in film production and photography that we bring to every project. We help our clients through the process of selecting and creating assets that perfectly convey their message. It can be as large as a documentary or as small as a seasonal photo shoot, we'll guide you through it.
